The important role of
a fieldworker

Fieldworkers play one of the most crucial roles in the Bohemia project because you make the first, and often, only contact with the study participants. A major part of your job is contacting community members and enlisting the participation in this project. The success of the study largely depends on how you approach people and work with them while they complete the questionnaire. We would like all of the study participants to complete the entire survey and give honest answers. Thus, your ability to patiently motivate respondents to answer the questions in a thoughtful way and accurately input the data is crticial to making sure this survey is a success in your region.

The Bohemia team is grateful for your contribution as a fieldworker! Please never hesitate to contact your supervisor or the project team if you have any questions or concerns.
